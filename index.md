 The Linguistic Landscapes of Beirut project (LLB) was a multilingual spatial humanities mapping project. Linguistic Landscape studies document and analyze how multilingualism is expressed in a written manner in public space.  The LLB project brought together some 2000 written language samples from the greater Beirut area captured in 2015/16.
