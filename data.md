 The Linguistic Landscapes of Beirut project (LLB) collected data in situ in 2015-16.

## The Dataset:

The full dataset, after dedoubling, contains some 2100 photos of languages samples taken in the greater urban area of Beirut, Lebanon. Of those photos, over 1300 were automatically geocoded. The aggregate of the points in greater Beirut are shown in the image below

<img src="/images/beirut_aggregate.png" style="zoom:50%;" />

The full extent of the dataset in the Republic of Lebanon is visualized [here](). 


## Method:

- Our article ["Digital Spatial Practices and Linguistic Landscaping in Beirut"](https://doi.org/10.4000/ilcea.9198) on LLB appeared in ILCEA Revue de l’Institut des langues et cultures d'Europe, Amérique, Afrique, Asie et Australie. It discusses extensively the method and challenges of LL mapping in the region.
